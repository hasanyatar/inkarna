Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

INKARNA (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a tattoo design and preview tool that lets you discover ideas, generate tattoo designs with AI, and preview them on your own skin. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. The App is operated by its developer, Hasan Yatar.

The short version. We collect only what the App needs to work: your account, the photos and designs you create, and basic purchase and diagnostic data. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

We request access to your camera (for live AR try-on) and your photo library (to upload images and save designs). These are used only for those features and only with your permission, which you can change anytime in iOS Settings.

2. How AI processing works

When you generate a design or preview a tattoo on your skin, the photo and the text prompt you provide are sent securely to our AI processing provider to produce your result. These images and prompts are used solely to generate the result you requested. We do not sell them and we do not use your photos to advertise to you.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the core features: design generation, lettering, and on-skin preview.
  • To create, secure, and sync your account and library.
  • To process purchases and manage your subscription and credits.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse (for example, of free starter credits).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.
  • To respond to your support requests.

4. Service providers we share with

We use trusted third parties only to operate the App. They process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, file storage, cloud functions, push messaging, crash reporting, and configuration.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management.
  • fal.ai — AI image generation (processes your photos and prompts to create results).
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, App Store payments, and DeviceCheck.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for cross-app advertising or tracking.

5. Data retention

We keep your account and content for as long as your account is active. Diagnostic data is retained only as long as needed to fix issues. When you delete your account (see below), your personal data and content are deleted, except where we must keep limited records to comply with legal or tax obligations.

6. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the App (Profile → account settings → delete account). You may also email us at inkarna.app@gmail.com and we will delete your data. Some purchase records may be retained as required by law.

7. Children’s privacy

INKARNA is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored with reputable providers using industry-standard safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and the CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at inkarna.app@gmail.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising them.

10. International data transfers

Our providers may process and store data in countries other than yours, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for significant changes, provide notice within the App.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at inkarna.app@gmail.com.
